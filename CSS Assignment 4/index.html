The Fantastic Tales of Mr. Peanut

There once was a little peanut named Johnny. Johnny was a brave, courageous peanut but unfortunately he suffered from anxiety and panic attacks. One day when Johnny was walking home he heard screaming. Johnny ran towards the screaming and saw it was coming from a house that was on fire. Without batting an eye Johnny dashed into the house to save the person he had heard screaming. Johnny found the person and managed to save both them and himself. The town saw Johnny's brave deeds and decided to award him with a bravery medal. Johnny was so ecstatic he started crying.

Characteristics Of Johnny

  • Brave
  • Shy
  • Anxious

The Steps Johnny Took To Save The Person

  1. Ran into the house
  2. Identified which room the screaming the was originating from
  3. Ran toward the room
  4. Calmed down the person
  5. Told the person to try and keep their head low or crawl if need be to reduce smoke inhalation
  6. Escaped the home with the person only suffering mild burns
